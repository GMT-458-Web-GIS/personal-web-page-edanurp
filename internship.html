Tapu Ve Kadastro Genel Müdürlüğü

The Directorate of Land Registry and Cadastre is a government institution that provides public services regarding the registration, monitoring and protection of ownership of real estate. This institution ensures that all real estate-related transactions are carried out in an orderly and safe manner.

Tasks:

  • Determination and registration of ownership of real estate
  • Determining the boundaries of real estate and creating maps.
  • Carrying out transactions such as mortgage, transfer, sale and acquisition.
  • Execution of expropriation processes.

Sapmaz Havacılık


In this project, I used remote sensing data to assess plant health. I performed disease analysis by calculating GNDVI, NDRE, LCI and NDWI indices.

Tasks:

  • Performing analyses by extracting NDVI map from the QGIS platform
  • Creating vegetation indices using the QGIS platform
  • Compare maps from different dates

Tübitak Uzay Teknolojileri Araştırma Enstitüsü Müdürlüğü


The goal of this project is to analyze the spatial pattern and density of events in a given area (e.g., crime rates, facility locations, customer distributions) and determine whether the data is random, clustered, or regularly distributed.

Tasks:

  • Performing point pattern analysis
  • Interpretation of data analysis results
  • Mapping and visualization
